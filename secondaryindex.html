Whenever someone brings up the ethnic group known as Creole in conversation it is met with a myriad of responses. Some people have a very vague definition or idea pertaining to the subject while others just flat out have no idea what you are talking about. While the latter tends to happen more often unfortunately, when it comes to people who know of the ethnic group misinformation is worse than no information at all.
